SCAREDY PLANTS

LEAF ME ALONE

WHAT IT IS

A small robotic kinetic sculpture designed to react to its surroundings in a way that reads as timidity to people watching and interacting with it. It will look like a potted plant with small legs allowing it to scuttle slowly around, moving towards light. If its surroundings grow too loud or otherwise alarming, the plant robot will stop moving and fold its leaves into itself so it shrinks in appearance.

OUR INSPIRATIONS

Shameplant
We are inspired by biology, kinetic art, and human interaction with robots. We liked the motion of the ‘shameplant’, so called because it folds up its leaves and shrinks away when touched. One of our main goals was to evoke emotion with our robot in a non-obvious way, and to have our project interact with the environment around it.​
